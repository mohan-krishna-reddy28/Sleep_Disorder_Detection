Symptoms of Common Sleep Disorders

Insomnia
  • Difficulty falling asleep
  • Frequent night awakenings
  • Waking up too early
  • Daytime sleepiness & fatigue
Sleep Apnea
  • Loud snoring
  • Pauses in breathing during sleep
  • Morning headaches
  • Daytime tiredness & poor concentration

Why Sleep Monitoring Matters?

Brain Health

Quality sleep improves memory, learning, and emotional stability. Monitoring helps detect patterns affecting mental performance.

Prevent Health Risks

Sleep disorders can lead to hypertension, diabetes, and heart conditions. Early detection helps avoid long-term complications.

Continuous Monitoring

With AI-based systems like NeuroSleep, continuous sleep analysis provides insights and improves the accuracy of diagnosis.

Tips for Better Sleep

Improve Your Sleep Routine
  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule
  • Avoid caffeine after evening
  • Reduce screen time before bed
  • Keep your room dark & quiet
Stay Physically Active
  • Exercise at least 30 minutes daily
  • Avoid heavy meals before bedtime
  • Walk regularly to reduce stress
Manage Stress & Mind
  • Practice deep breathing
  • Try meditation or yoga
  • Reduce nighttime anxiety
